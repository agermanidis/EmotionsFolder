THE EMOTIONS FOLDER

automatically archives everything you feel in front of your screen.

Download (OS X only)

Check out the source code!

The Emotions Folder is an OS X app that uses your webcam to continuously monitor your facial expression. When it recognizes a display of emotion (happiness, sadness, anger, or surprise), it takes a GIF snapshot of your face alongside with what’s on your screen and stores it inside a folder named “Emotions” in your home directory.
